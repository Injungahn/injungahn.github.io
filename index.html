Projects

Yelp Review Categorizer

NLP, Webscraping, Sentiment Analysis

For my Data Science Immersive capstone I webscraped Yelp for reviews from 50 restaurants in San Francisco. I gathered stopwords by lemmatizing all of the sentences from the reviews. I then used those stopwords to classify each of the sentences into food, service, and timing. After classifying the sentences I ran sentiment analysis to create scores for each of the categories to further assist choosing restaurants.
